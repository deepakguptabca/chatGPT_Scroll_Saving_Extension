**Steps to install in your chrome**

Step 1 : Download the zip file.
Step 2 : Extract into a folder.
Step 3 : Go to extensions and enable developer mode.
Step 4 : Click on **Load Unpacked** and select the downloaded extension folder.
Step 5 : Click on OK and your extension is successfully installed to your chrome.
Step 6 : Click once on the extension and it will enabled.
Step 7 : Now Save your time from scrolling and enjoy :)
